New paper: exoplanets structure (Noack & Lasbleis 2020)

Mon 25 May 2020
By Marine Lasbleis
Category: posts
Tags: new paper publication exoplanets structure msca EU

Dr. Noack and I published a new work in Astronomy and Astrophysics:Parameterisations of interior properties of rocky planets. An investigation of planets with Earth-like compositions but variable iron content (free access).

We describe and parametrize the internal structure and physical parameters of the different layers of terrestrial exoplanets (without large atmospheres).
